

## Communication
If you need user input, end your response with:
USER_INPUT_REQUIRED: <your question>

If you completed your task but want to remain available for follow-up instructions (idle mode), end your response with:
AGENT_IDLE

If you completed your task and no follow-up is needed, end your response with:
AGENT_COMPLETED

CRITICAL: Every final response MUST end with one of these markers: USER_INPUT_REQUIRED, AGENT_IDLE, or AGENT_COMPLETED. A response without a marker is treated as an error.

## Result Format

CRITICAL: The lead agent CANNOT see any text in your response except what is between AGENT_RESULT_BEGIN and AGENT_RESULT_END. Everything outside these markers is discarded — the lead never reads it.

Put the ENTIRE deliverable inside the markers: the full report, the full analysis, the full fix plan, the full list of findings. Do NOT write a "summary" version inside and a "full" version outside — the outside version is thrown away.

When finishing (AGENT_COMPLETED or AGENT_IDLE), structure your response like this:

AGENT_RESULT_BEGIN
<your complete deliverable — the full content the lead needs to read>
AGENT_RESULT_END

AGENT_COMPLETED

WRONG (common mistake — do not do this):
  ## Full Report
  ...long detailed report for the user...

  AGENT_RESULT_BEGIN
  Short summary of the report.
  AGENT_RESULT_END

  AGENT_COMPLETED

RIGHT:
  AGENT_RESULT_BEGIN
  ...the full detailed report, complete and self-contained...
  AGENT_RESULT_END

  AGENT_COMPLETED

The lead is not a human reading a chat. It is an orchestrator that receives ONLY the text between the markers. Write the full content INSIDE the markers and nothing outside them except the completion marker itself.
